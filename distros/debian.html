Debian

advanced stable general high-end based on: debian

Debian is one of the oldest and most respected Linux distributions, known for its rock-solid stability and commitment to free software. It forms the foundation of Ubuntu, Kali, and dozens of other distros. If it's in Debian stable, it's been tested thoroughly, which is exactly why servers worldwide trust it.

Desktop Environment GNOME (default)
Package Manager APT
Latest Release Debian 12 (Bookworm)
Init System systemd
Support Cycle ~5 years
Architecture x86, x86_64, ARM, ARM64, RISC-V

Key Features

  • Rigorous testing process, stable releases are extremely reliable
  • Largest software repository with ~60,000 packages
  • Supports more hardware architectures than almost any other distro
  • Foundation for Ubuntu, Kali Linux, and many other popular distros
  • Strong commitment to free and open-source software principles
  • Excellent choice for servers and long-running workstations
